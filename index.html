Folia Smart: Science of Vegetation

About Us

We are a group of researchers committed to creating Internet of Things (IoT) based solutions for sustainable vegetation growth, and reduced water use. Our aim is to have systems that respond to changing climatic conditions while helping preserve plant diversity.

Things We Make

Our philosophy is to use inexpensive yet powerful IoT platforms to create easy to use, smart, solar-powered systems that monitor vegetation through visible-range/infrared cameras and other sensors. Assisted by distributed computing and machine learning, these systems would work with minimal human interaction to control water use for desired vegetation growth. Through data-sharing, we hope to integrate local/indigenous/community practices of working sustainably with the biosphere within the knowledgebase.
